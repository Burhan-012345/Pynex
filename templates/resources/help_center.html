Help Center

Find answers to common questions and learn how to make the most of Pynex

Getting Started

How do I create my first expense?

To create your first expense, click on "Add Expense" in the dashboard, fill in the amount, category, and description, then click "Save".

Can I import data from other apps?

Currently, we support CSV imports. Go to Settings → Import Data to upload your expense files from other applications.

How do I set up budgets?

Budget features are coming in our next update! You'll be able to set monthly budgets for different categories and receive alerts.

Account & Settings

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

Can I use Pynex on multiple devices?

Yes! Pynex syncs your data across all your devices. Just log in with your account on any device.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Please note this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Features & Functionality

How does the AI insights feature work?

Our AI analyzes your spending patterns and provides personalized recommendations to help you save money and optimize your budget.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export your expense data as CSV or PDF from the Reports section of your dashboard.

Are there mobile apps available?

Mobile apps for iOS and Android are currently in development and will be released in early 2024.

Our support team is here to assist you with any questions or issues.
